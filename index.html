Foodie's Favorite

A beet sandwich typically features roasted or pickled beets as the main ingredient, providing a sweet and earthy flavor. The sandwich can be assembled in various ways, often including components like goat cheese or feta, arugula or other greens, and a spread such as hummus or a citrusy vinaigrette to balance the beet's natural sweetness. The bread used can range from hearty whole grain to a crusty baguette, depending on personal preference. This sandwich is popular among those looking for a vegetarian option that's both nutritious and flavorful.
